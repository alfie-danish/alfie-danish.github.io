About Me

Hi there! I'm Alfie, a data enthusiast and lifelong learner with a passion for reading fantasy fiction and multiplayer gaming. Currently, I work as a People Tech Analyst at a FinTech, where I specialize in Workday HRIS system, HR business processes and people data. I'm always looking for ways to uncover insights and make sense of complex information.

My fascination with data started with Python, and I have since developed skills in data visualization, data analytics and data science. I'm always looking for new ways to apply my skills and expertise to help organizations achieve their goals.

Beyond my work, I love exploring new programming languages and tools. I'm currently learning JavaScript and App Script, excited to expand my knowledge and stay up-to-date with the latest trends and technologies.

When I'm not working with data or programming, you can find me immersed in a good book or battling it out with friends in a multiplayer game. I believe in staying curious and engaged, and I'm always looking for new opportunities to learn and grow.

Thank you for visiting my website. If you're interested in collaborating on a project or learning more about my work, please feel free to reach out, or check out my projects below.
